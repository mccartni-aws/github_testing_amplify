How Does This Work?

In today’s fast paced world, you’ve got places you need to be but not enough time in your jam packed schedule to buy the ingredients you need. That's where AWSome-B2 come in, as we hope to provide you with the exact ingredients needed to create a meal of your choice. Getting started is as easy as tapping a button in our app.

Download The App

Head over to the app store and download the AWSome-B2 app. You’re just a few taps away from getting a suitable recipe.

Fill in our ingredients form

We can get you there. Simply let us know what you've got in your fridge!.

Pick A Price

Pick the amount you're willing to spend on food and we will suggest the best recipes, based on your budget and your current fridge stock.

Get ready to eat!

After selecting the meal you want, we will suggest which shops are closest to you, so that you can go and collect them.
